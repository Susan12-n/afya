Symptoms

1.Fever and Chills: Often occurring in cycles, 2.high fever with alternating chills is one of the hallmark symptoms. 3.Sweating: As the fever breaks, heavy sweating can follow. 4.Headache: Persistent and sometimes severe headache. 5.Fatigue and Weakness: Feeling extremely tired and weak is common. 6.Muscle and Joint Pain: Body aches, similar to flu symptoms, may occur. 7.Nausea and Vomiting: Some people may experience gastrointestinal symptoms. 8.Abdominal Pain: Pain in the stomach or abdomen can sometimes occur. 9.Anemia: Malaria can cause a drop in red blood cells, leading to anemia and related symptoms like dizziness and pale skin. 10.Enlarged Spleen: Some may have a tender, enlarged spleen upon examination.
